Blog #9

Turing the week, I was able to make phone calls to professionals in order to further gain knowledge on my topic of UI/UX development. I reached out to experienced UI/UX designers who generously shared their insights and experiences, giving me valuable advice on best practices and current trends in the field. With the help of the phone calls and the emails that I sent out to professionals in this field, I was able to gather a comprehensive understanding of the latest tools and software used in UI/UX development. Additionally, these professionals also provided me with recommendations for relevant online courses and workshops that could enhance my skills and keep me updated with the ever-evolving industry. The feedback and guidance from these professionals not only helped me improve my technical skills but also provided me with valuable insights into the industry's best practices and trends.

As a result, I was able to showcase my enhanced abilities through my updated portfolio website and successfully apply them to create a visually appealing and user-friendly website for my school club. The link to the website where I used the recommendations from the professionals is podagamedaygrill.com. This project not only uses the skills that I learned from those professionals, but it also allowed me to gain hands-on experience in web development and project management. By working on a real-world project for my school club, I was able to apply the knowledge and techniques I acquired from industry professionals in a practical setting. This experience not only solidified my understanding of web development principles but also enhanced my ability to effectively collaborate with a team and meet deadlines.
