Blog #8

During the Thanksgiving break, I was able to do more research about UI/UX and how it can help me with my final project. I discovered various online resources and tutorials that provided valuable insights into the principles of UI/UX design. Additionally, I reached out to professionals in the field who shared their experiences and offered guidance on incorporating effective UI/UX elements into my final project. I reached out to someone who works at Adobe and has an interest in digital experience which is user experience. They provided me with valuable advice on how to create a seamless user journey and emphasized the importance of user testing to ensure optimal functionality. Their expertise and insights have greatly influenced my approach to UI/UX design, and I am excited to implement their suggestions in my final project.

The research I did also helped me to start brainstorming more content that I can put on my original work. Additionally, I discovered that conducting user research and gathering feedback from potential users is crucial in identifying pain points and areas for improvement in the UI/UX design. This process allowed me to gain a deeper understanding of my target audience's needs and preferences, enabling me to tailor my final project accordingly. Moreover, exploring various design patterns and studying successful UI/UX examples has sparked new ideas and inspired me to think creatively when it comes to presenting content in an engaging and intuitive manner.
