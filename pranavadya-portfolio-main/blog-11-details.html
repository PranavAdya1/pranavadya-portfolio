Blog #11

This week I was able to start planning what I am going to do for my final product. For my original work I made the wireframe/prototype of what the website is going to look like, but now I have to actually make the website and plan out all of the content that is going to be on it. I have decided to use a combination of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring my website to life. Additionally, I am considering incorporating interactive features and engaging visuals to enhance the user experience.

For example, I am deciding to put interactive quizzes to help quiz the people on my website about elements of UI/UX. By incorporating interactive quizzes, I aim to not only engage users but also test their understanding of UI/UX concepts. This will not only make the website more interactive but also provide a valuable learning experience for visitors. Additionally, I plan to utilize engaging visuals such as animations and illustrations to further enhance the overall aesthetic appeal of the website and make it visually appealing to users.

In conclusion, I was able to start planning out my final product with the help of my original work. With the help of my future mentor, I will be able to speed up this process and hopefully create a very good website for my users.
