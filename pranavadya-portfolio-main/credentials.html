About Me

My name is Pranav Adya, and I am a student in the Frisco Independent School District. More specifically, I am currently a junior at Centennial High School. I am interested in the field of UI/UX and have been learning more about this topic through a variety of courses and projects. My interest in UI/UX was mainly due to the fact that UI/UX is the meeting point of design and technology and how they can be used together to make user friendly interfaces.

I hope to pursue a career in the field of UI/UX in the future to help contribute to the development of good user experiences on products such as websites and mobile applications.
