Web Development for ATYHS

Apex Texas Youth Honor Society

ATYHS

During my time as the web manager for Apex Texas Youth Honor Society, I was tasked with creating a website that displays everything we have achieved. The website includes multiple pictures of different events we have planned and volunteered at. For example, we have done litter cleanups at many different parks in the Plano, Texas, area, where we worked together to make a positive impact on the environment. Additionally, the website also showcases our participation in community service projects such as food drives and clothing donations.

About ATYHS

Apex Texas Youth Honor Society, or ATYHS in short, is a non-profit volunteer organization in Plano and Allen, Texas. Our main goal is to motivate students, from middle school to high school, to make the world a better place by starting with the small local community. ATYHS provides various opportunities for students to engage in meaningful community service projects and develop leadership skills. Through our initiatives, we aim to instill a sense of responsibility and compassion in young individuals, empowering them to create positive change not only in their immediate surroundings but also on a larger scale.
