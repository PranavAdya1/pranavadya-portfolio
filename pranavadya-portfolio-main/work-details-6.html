Rocket Testing

My group and I built a rocket and competed with other groups in the area to see who would have the best design and highest launch based on different criteria. We spent weeks researching different rocket designs, testing prototypes, and fine-tuning our launch technique to ensure our rocket performed at its best during the competition. The experience taught us valuable lessons in teamwork, problem-solving, and perseverance that we will carry with us in future projects. The competition was a thrilling and challenging experience that pushed us to think creatively and work efficiently under pressure.
