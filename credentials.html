About Me

My name is Pranav Adya, and I am a student in the Frisco Independent School District. More specifically, I am currently a junior at Centennial High School. My interest in UI/UX was mainly due to the fact that UI/UX is the meeting point of design and technology and how they can be used together to make user friendly interfaces.
