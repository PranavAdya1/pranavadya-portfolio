During the week, I was able to fix up my presentation for my ISM topic, UI/UX. During the week, I was able to fix up my presentation for my ISM topic, UI/UX. I spent several hours researching and gathering relevant information on UI/UX design principles, as well as analyzing successful case studies. Additionally, I incorporated engaging visuals and interactive elements to enhance the overall user experience during the presentation. I was also able to call some more professionals in the UI /UX field and conduct interviews to gain insights into current industry trends and best practices. I was also able to find courses on UIUX that I can take to further enhance my knowledge and skills in this area. Furthermore, I made sure to include real-life examples of successful user interface from popular apps and websites to illustrate the importance of user-centered design.

Lastly, I dedicated a section of my presentation to discuss the challenges faced by UI/UX designers and how they can be overcome using various strategies and techniques. I was also able to start the development process of my original work by creating wireframes and prototypes to visualize the user interface and gather feedback from potential users. Additionally, I conducted usability testing sessions to identify any usability issues and iterate on the design accordingly. This iterative process allowed me to refine my original work and ensure a seamless user experience.
