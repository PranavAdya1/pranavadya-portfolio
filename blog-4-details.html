Blog #4

This week in class, I was able to reflect on my experiences at the ISM professional symposium. During this time, I was able to meet many professionals in their respective fields and learn about them. I was particularly inspired by the keynote speaker, Denise Donoghue (The Mortgage Nerd), who shared her journey from her most difficult times to becoming a successful professional. Denise's story resonated with me as she emphasized the importance of perseverance and resilience in overcoming challenges. Her journey reminded me that setbacks are not permanent and can be stepping stones towards personal growth and success. Hearing her speak has motivated me to approach my own career with a renewed sense of determination and optimism.

This week I was also introduced to making a contact list for the professionals that I wish to call to set up informational interviews with. Creating a contact list will not only help me expand my network but also provide valuable insights and guidance from experienced professionals in the field of UI/UX. By reaching out to these individuals, I can gain a deeper understanding of the industry and potentially uncover new opportunities for personal and professional development. Finally, this week I was also able to learn about effective email templates that I can use to also help set up informational interviews with professionals in UI/UX. Overall, this week I was able to accomplish a lot in terms of expanding my network and gaining valuable knowledge in the field of UI/UX. With the insights and guidance from experienced professionals, I can increase my skills and have a clearer vision of my career path.
