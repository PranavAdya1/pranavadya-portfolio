Go-kart Design for a Summer Project

Summer Project

Go-kart

For this project, we decided to build everything from scrath, except for the engine. We built the frame, steering system, and did all of the welding for this project. Specifically, this go-kart has a 212cc engine that we purchased and installed to ensure optimal performance. Additionally, we designed the body of the go-kart to be lightweight yet durable, allowing for maximum speed and maneuverability. During this experience, we learned that the design process is very important in ensuring the functionality and efficiency of the final product. By customizing each component to fit our specific needs, we were able to create a go-kart that exceeded our expectations in terms of performance and reliability.
